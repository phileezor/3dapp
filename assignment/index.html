Statement of Originality


This 3D SPA web application is submitted as a requirement for the module of Web 3D Applications at the University of Sussex. It is the product of my own labour except where indicated.


About Cola Soda...


Cola Soda was developed with the purpose of showcasing a 3D application but also to show the deep admiration and fizzy nature of Coca Colas collection of sodas. This Web 3D Application is designed to fulfill most if not all of the requirements for the assignment. Enjoy and keep exploring.
